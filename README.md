## 📖 About credentials-manager

credentials-manager is a tool to help you store your passwords and other login information safely on your computer. It works completely offline inside your browser. You do not need to install anything or hand over your data to a server. It also supports two-factor authentication (2FA) codes, so you can manage your one-time passwords (TOTP) all in one place. You can add tags to your entries to keep them organized. You can also import or export your data to keep backups or move your information.

This app runs in any modern web browser on Windows without needing extra software. It keeps your data private and secure by saving everything locally on your device.

## ⚙️ How to Use credentials-manager

### Add credentials

1. Open the app in your browser (open the index.html file).
2. Click “Add New Credential.”
3. Fill in the site name, username, and password.
4. Add optional tags to organize your entries.
5. Save the credential.
6. To add a 2FA code, enter the secret key the site provides.
7. The app will show your TOTP codes when needed.

### View and manage credentials

- Use the search bar to find credentials by site or tag.
- Click an item to see details or edit it.
- Delete old or unused entries anytime.

### Import and export data

- Export your data as a file to keep a backup.
- Import from a previously saved file to restore your credentials.

## 💡 Tips for Best Use

- Always use a strong password for your device and browser profile.
- Back up your exported data to a secure place.
- Use 2FA whenever possible for better security.
- Keep the index.html file in a safe folder on your computer.
- If you clear your browser data, your local credentials may be lost, so keep a backup.
